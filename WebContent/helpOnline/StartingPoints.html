EasyPaymentSystem, Card Management System a set of products modular, that’s follows and processes all type of transactions; it has been designed first to fight all frauds or mistakes linked to a card transaction according to a set of international rules often specific by product/network brand, second to manage a very huge volume of transactions on a very optimize time and allow big online availability, third secure all operations users or transactional.


EPS solution is appropriate for any business that consists in processing electronic payment transactions, with a broad array of services relating to consulting and audit, implementation and maintenance, training and permanent support.




EPS is an application to manage the electronic payment using all the new concept required from all the international networks, it has the latest functionalities that the banks used in managing their debit/credit cards.



* Host Module, in order to provide optimized rules in the definition of banks customer and the management of there accounts when paid by the debit cards.
* Host Interface, in order to provide an online tool allowing to follow quickly and accurately any customer request of payment, withdrawal money and ATM services (such as balance inquiry, short statement, transfer, bill payment)
* Issuing: A module to manage a banks card. Including management of cardholder agreements, management of all types production card(Emv, Internet, Magstrip), management of all types card(Debit, Credit, Prepaid, Installement), maker checker management, following of delivery cards and Pin and so on
* Acquiring: in order to manage the pack of Merchant acquired by the bank and offer all the services related this management (such as discount, settlement)
* Risk: in order to provide banks with innovative system that offers an efficient solution to fight Card and Merchant frauds
* Security: in order to provide banks with innovative system that offers an efficient solution to strength the security of system access and controls (such as, a very strong user password, control card security (Pin, Cvv), Management of Audit trail)
* Transactions: in order to provide banks with innovative system that offers an efficient solution to collect all type of transactions coming from different channels (Pos collection, Atm cutover, International incoming, captured transaction)
* Dispute, (Charge-Backs, unpaid, contested transactions) in order to provide them with a software allowing to manage their daily work according to priority and timing rules defined by the international organizations such as Visa, MasterCard, Diners, Amex,
* ATM NDC server, in order to provide the bank by a switch NDC server to manage the bank’s machines
* POS server, in order to provide the bank by a switch POS server to manage the bank’s machines
* Accounting, in order to reconcile and control the financial flow
* Loyalty, a module that makes possible to manage a structured marketing efforts that reward, and therefore encourage, loyal buying behavior which is potentially of benefit to the bank.
* Statistics, a module that makes possible to have an over view of the system performances and business activities, that the bank can use to put a marketing strategy.
* ISO8583 Interfaces, in order to allow interfacing with other systems.
